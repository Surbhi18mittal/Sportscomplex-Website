ABOUT US

Peoria Sports Complex is a sports stadium in Chandigarh,India. The stadium was established in 2012 and is owned by Chandigarh Urban Development Authority.The stadium is primer ports venue in the city with capacity of 8,000 with international standard facilities The stadium has got facilities for various sports like cricket, football, hockey etc. There are also facilities for indoor sports such as basketball, badminton, gymnastics, handball, volleyball, lawn tennis, table tennis, weight lifting and Kabbadi etc.
